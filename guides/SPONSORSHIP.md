# Sponsorship

Sponsorship for Terra Draw plays a vital role in sustaining the long-term health and growth of the project. By supporting its development, sponsors enable continuous improvements, new features, testing, and ongoing maintenance across the project. This backing helps ensure Terra Draw can remain a reliable, well-maintained, and forward-looking open-source mapping library. With sponsorship, more time can be dedicated to innovation, documentation, community support — ultimately helping Terra Draw thrive and evolve for years to come.

## Sponsoring

Terra Draw is primarily maintained by James Milner. You can find his [GitHub sponsor page here](https://github.com/sponsors/JamesLMilner).
